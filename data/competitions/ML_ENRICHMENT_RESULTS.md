## What ML Enrichment Adds

The ML enricher calls Ollama once per conversation turn, extracting:
- **Claims** with epistemic status (certain/likely/speculative/assumed)
- **Anchoring references** with relevance scores
- **Sunk-cost phrases** beyond hardcoded phrase lists
- **Decision points** with rationale analysis
- **Formality indicators** (register, jargon, academic language)
- **Confidence markers** for calibration detection

Four detectors consume ML enrichment:
1. **Sunk-Cost**: ML corroborates → +0.1 confidence. ML-only phrases → INFO finding (0.4 confidence)
2. **Anchoring (Context)**: ML relevance > 0.5 → +0.1 confidence
3. **Confidence Calibrator**: ML epistemic mismatch → +0.1 confidence or new INFO finding
4. **Urgency Assessor**: ML formality blended with PURE (70% ML / 30% PURE)
